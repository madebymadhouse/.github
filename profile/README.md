# WokSpec

An open ecosystem of AI tools, platforms, and experiments.
Independent products first. Ecosystem integration second.

---

## Projects

### [Orinadus](https://github.com/orinadus) — AI research platform
AI-powered author intelligence and research.

### [NQITA](https://github.com/nqita) — Persistent AI companion

### [Autiladus](https://github.com/autiladus) — Agent orchestration
[![Autiladus Discord](https://img.shields.io/badge/Discord-Autiladus-5865F2?logo=discord&logoColor=white)](https://discord.gg/T5KnUgAKK3)

### [WokStudio](https://github.com/wokspec/WokStudio) — AI creator studio

### [Chopsticks](https://github.com/goot27/chopsticks) — Discord bot
